About Our Club

As Neil DeGrass Tyson said "The good thing about science is that it's true whether or not you believe in it".

The aim of this club is to spread awareness among college students regarding the importance of space science research. After observing the current situation, we concluded that students needs to be aware about the vastness of this universe so that they can get a whole new perspective towards their life.
